Georgia My Voter Page (MVP)
Last reviewed: April 16, 2026

The Georgia Secretary of State describes My Voter Page as the statewide portal to:
- access and update voter information
- check registration status
- locate advance-voting and Election Day polling locations
- view a sample ballot
- view elected officials

Georgia early-voting guidance on the same page says:
- Georgia voters have three weeks before Election Day for early voting
- counties can offer Sunday voting
- voters can use any early-voting location in their county

Georgia absentee-voting guidance says:
- no-excuse absentee voting is available
- voters can request an absentee ballot online or by mail

Use the live MVP site and Georgia Secretary of State elections pages for current statewide voting rules and polling-location lookup.
