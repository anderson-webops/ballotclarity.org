Fulton County Polling and Precinct Tools
Last reviewed: April 16, 2026

Fulton County directs voters to its polling-tools page for:
- precinct and polling-place lookup
- election-day location checks
- links to Georgia My Voter Page

The county page tells voters to use Georgia My Voter Page to confirm:
- voter registration status
- Election Day polling place
- sample ballot

Practical use:
Check the county polling-tools page and Georgia My Voter Page shortly before traveling, especially if you are relying on a polling-place assignment or need the latest election-day logistics.
