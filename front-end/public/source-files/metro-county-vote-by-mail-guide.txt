Metro County Vote-by-Mail and Ballot Return Guide Demo
Last updated: March 31, 2026

Requesting a ballot:
The county elections office accepts vote-by-mail requests through October 27, 2026 for the current election coverage.

Returning a ballot:
Ballots may be returned by mail, by secure county drop box, or in person at the elections office during posted hours.

Tracking:
The county guide describes a ballot tracking process that confirms when a mailed ballot is issued, received, and accepted for counting.

Important note:
Mail delivery times and ballot signature rules are governed by the county office and should be verified before relying on mailed return.

This is a reference source file included with the current Ballot Clarity coverage.
